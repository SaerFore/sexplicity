Sexplicity

Sexplicity is a production-led music project developed through the creative identities of Eli Ruffo and Saer Fore.

Swaycore

The project develops and defines swaycore, a groove-first alternative rock genre derived from swancore.

Swaycore preserves the technical and melodic guitar language of swancore, but changes its center of gravity. Bass movement, pocket-focused drums, physical repetition and expressive vocals guide the arrangement, while the guitars create rhythm, tension and memorable melodic detail.

Its sound moves between intimacy and controlled aggression, combining dark sensuality, danceable momentum and selective technical complexity.

Core characteristics

  • Groove as the main structural force.
  • Technical, melodic and rhythm-oriented guitars.
  • Active bass lines closely locked with the drums.
  • Organic, pocket-focused drumming with cuts and accents that respond to the vocal phrasing.
  • Expressive vocals that can move between clean intimacy, grit and force.
  • Slow-to-mid-tempo movement, physical repetition and controlled rhythmic tension.
  • Lyrics centered on intimacy, desire, emotional distance and human connection.

Discography

Body Language

A conceptual release built around physical communication, attraction and the things people express without saying them directly.

No Love Fest

A full-length release built around groove, melodic tension, intimacy and shifting emotional distance.

The foundations of swaycore were developed across Body Language and No Love Fest before the genre was formally named.
